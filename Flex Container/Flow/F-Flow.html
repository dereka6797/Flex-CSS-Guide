In regards to flex-flow it really is just a shorthand property for the following two flex properties: flex-direction & flex-wrap. It allows you to use those properties under flex-flow. For that reason alone I will not be showing any examples, simply because it will just be no different than going over the direction and wrap sections. I will however give the syntax below:

flex-flow: 'flex-direction' OR 'flex-wrap' | INITIAL | INHERIT;
